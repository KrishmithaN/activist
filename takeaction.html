What can I do to support this cause?

There are a number of ways that YOU can contribute to providing women with free menstrual products.

1. Sign, Lead and Share Petitions

Signing petitions is just like voting for a President. Every vote counts. Petitions are a great way to show the state that free menstrual products is a NEED. There are a number of petitions online that you can sign. Don't forget to share these petitions as well so more people are aware of the issue. Below are just a few petitions you can sign. Feel free to visit the Resources tab for more.

2. Donate menstrual and hygiene products

Another way you can help is by donating unused and clean menstrual and hygiene products. Menstrual products include tampons, pads, and menstrual cups. Hygiene products include clean underwear, sanitary napkins, and cloth. Reach out to a local womens shelter and see if they take donations. Please remember that if you donate any of these products, make sure they are clean, unused and packed.

3. Donate money

And lastly, you can donate money. Similar to the last option, reach out to a local or any shelter and ask if they take donation towards womens shelters. If you are having a hard time contacting any, you can also donate through petitions and other websites that collect money for womens menstrual products. Below are a few links that you can donate to. Feel free to visit the Resources page for more.

4. Share, Share, Share

The biggest way to send a message out is to have a numerous amount of people being aware of the situation. No one can help if no one knows. Even if you are not able to help in any way, share what you see. You can share this website, petitions and donation links to spread the message.
